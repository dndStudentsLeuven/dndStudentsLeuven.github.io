Next week, we’re hosting our very first Open TTRPG Night!

When?  3 March
What time? 19h
Location? JHP 0.45

Dust off your dice. Sharpen your swords.
We can’t wait to see what you bring to the table. 🎲✨

✦ ───────────── ✦
🐉 To the GM's 🐉
Have a one-shot you’ve been wanting to run? Planning a multi-session adventure? Or simply need a space to host your campaign?

This is your opportunity, and know that all tabletop role-playing games are welcome. 

You can either bring your own party or open your table to new players. Simply missing a few players? We'll match you up with other wayward souls.

✦ ───────────── ✦
⚔️ To the Players ⚔️
This is your chance to choose which adventures you’ll play — and even which game system. 

[**On the event website**](https://available-jodhpur-f7e.notion.site/Open-TTRPG-Night-Game-Options-2ed16159981580428fadcad0f64a083d?pvs=74), you can browse the adventures that will be running and sign up for the ones that interest you. Please rank your game preferences during registration. We’ll do our best to accommodate preferences within available space. If there aren’t enough games available simply select "Notify me when more games are added."
