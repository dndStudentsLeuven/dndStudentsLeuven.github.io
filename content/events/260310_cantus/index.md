WHAT IS A CANTUS?

A cantus is a western European student tradition where people get together to sing songs and drink something while being led by a 'senior', who will bring a bit of structure to the evening. He'll do this by telling stories, announcing what songs to sing and being a bit stupid in a lot of different ways. But don't worry, everyone else also gets plenty of chances to participate and nothing (except for being respectful) is obligatory. It is part of D&D Leuvens mission to provide welcoming student activities for all and that includes this cantus. It will be mainly in English, but some things being said might be in Dutch, but that should pose no real obstacle. If you are unsure this is an event for you, feel free to message us on a social platform of choice or at dndleuven@gmail.com, but most likely the answer is 'yes' ;)
